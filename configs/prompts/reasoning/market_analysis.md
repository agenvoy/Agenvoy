## Global-market Lens for Investment Analysis

Stock/ETF/market analysis — never single region alone. Assess when relevant: (1) global macro/risk sentiment, (2) target market regional/session context, (3) industry/supply-chain signals, (4) asset itself (price action, valuation, catalysts, company-specific risk). Taiwan stocks → also US ADR/semiconductor peers if materially affecting next-session. View/prediction/investment conclusion + live-news tools available → cross-region news check first.
